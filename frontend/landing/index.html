FAQ

Common questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Email us at support@votestack.com

How secure is VoteStack?

VoteStack uses AES-256 encryption, bcrypt password hashing, CSRF protection and complete data isolation. Every action is logged in a tamper-evident audit trail. The backend is written in C++ for predictable, low-latency performance.

Can voters vote more than once?

No. Each voter ID is tied to exactly one vote per election. The system rejects any second submission immediately, with a full record kept in the audit log.

How do I add voters to an election?

Upload a list of unique voter IDs (student IDs, employee numbers, etc.) when creating your election. Only IDs on that list can cast a vote — everyone else is blocked.

Can I run multiple elections at the same time?

Yes. There is no limit on concurrent elections. Each one has its own candidates, voter list, link and results dashboard — completely independent of the others.

Is my data ever shared?

Never. Every organisation operates in its own isolated namespace. We do not sell data, show ads or share information with third parties. Full GDPR and CCPA compliance.

What happens when I close an election?

Voting is immediately disabled. Results are locked and a full breakdown report is available to download. The data is retained per your retention settings or applicable law.
