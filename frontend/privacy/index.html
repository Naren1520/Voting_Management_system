Introduction

VoteStack ("we", "our", or "us") operates an online voting platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information. Please read it carefully. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you give us directly, including:

  • Account details — name, email address, hashed password
  • Election data — titles, candidate names, voter ID lists
  • Voting records — participation status and vote timestamps
  • Profile preferences and settings

Automatically Collected Information

When you access the platform we automatically collect:

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Page visit timestamps and navigation paths
  • Error logs and diagnostic data

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our voting services
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraudulent voting
  • Process elections, record votes, and deliver results
  • Send security alerts and administrative notices
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform
  • Comply with applicable laws and enforce our terms
  • Respond to support requests

Data Protection & GDPR Compliance

We are committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent data protection laws. As a data subject you hold the following rights:

  • Right to Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Request limited processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability — Request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object — Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@votestack.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Complete data isolation between organisations
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits
  • Automated backup and disaster recovery systems
  • DDoS protection and real-time infrastructure monitoring

While we strive to protect your information, no transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to notifying you of any breach without undue delay.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data — retained while your account is active
  • Election and voter data — per your settings plus any legal requirements
  • Voting records — retained for audit and compliance purposes
  • Server logs — typically 90 days for security purposes

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time, subject to legal obligations.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We share information only in these limited cases:

  • With Your Consent — When you explicitly authorise sharing
  • Service Providers — Trusted vendors under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements — When required by law or valid government order
  • Business Transfers — In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • Rights Protection — To enforce our terms or protect user safety

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies strictly for platform operation:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for authentication and core functionality
  • Security Cookies — Protect against fraudulent activity
  • Performance Cookies — Help us understand usage patterns to improve the platform

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards — including standard contractual clauses — are in place for all such transfers in compliance with applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is not directed at users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Privacy Choices

  • Update your account details at any time in your profile settings
  • Request a copy of your personal data by contacting us
  • Delete your account and associated data via account settings
  • Opt out of non-essential communications via email preferences
  • Control cookies through your browser settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy here and updating the "Last updated" date. For significant changes we will also send an email notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
